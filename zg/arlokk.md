# High Priest Arlokk
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

This fight begins with Arlokk in Troll form. After 30 seconds, Arlokk will transform into her Panther form and vanish.

Throughout the fight, Arlokk will spawn panther adds. They should be ignored if Arlokk is alive, as damage is better served on her, but when she's vanished you can switch to clearing out the accumulated adds.

If killed slowly, Arlokk will begin switching back and forth between forms.

Here are the abilities to watch for:

### Troll Form

* **[Gouge](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=12540/gouge)**: Stuns the main tank for 4 sec. This requires a taunt swap.

### Panther Form

* **[Mark of Arlokk](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24210/mark-of-arlokk)**: From stealth, Arlokk goads panthers to swarm the targeted player.
* **[Whirlwind](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24236/whirlwind)**: Massive AoE damage for 2 seconds; melee run out, then back in.
