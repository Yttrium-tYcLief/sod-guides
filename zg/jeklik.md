# High Priest Jeklik
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

Very simple fight.

Here are the abilities to watch for:

## Bat Form (Phase 1)

* **[Pierce Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=12097)**: Armor reduction on the tank, heal through it.
* **[Swoop](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=23919)**: Frontal cone; keep the boss turned away from the raid.
* **[Curse of Blood](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=16098)**: Extremely minor curse. Decurse if you feel like it.
* **[Sonic Burst](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=23918)**: Damaging AoE that also silences for 10 seconds. Only a 20yd range, so stay max range for the fight.
* **[Blood Leech](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=22644)**: Life drain that multiplicatively heals Jeklik. Just hurt her faster.
* **[Charge](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=22911)**: She will occasionally charge at random enemies, stunning them for 2sec.
* **Summon Bats**: She'll occasionally summon bats, these should be picked up by an OT and dragged under the boss to prevent silencing casters.

## Troll Form (Phase 2)

* **[Great Heal](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=25807/great-heal)**: Heals her for ~25% health. Must be interrupted.
* **[Mind Flay](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=23953/mind-flay)**: Randomly targeted at the raid, does minimal damage.
* **Bat Bombers**: Bat bombers will drop fire bombs on the raid. Just avoid the fire patches when they occur.
