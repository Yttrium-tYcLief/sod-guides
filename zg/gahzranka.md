# Gahzranka
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

{: .important .fs-3}
Activating this encounter requires a **[Mudskunk Lure](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19974/mudskunk-lure)**.

Summoned from the abandonwed camp by the water northeast of Hakkar, southeast of Arlokk. Requires [a small attunement quest](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/quest=8227/nats-measuring-tape) and high level Fishing to summon.

Before pulling, everyone in the raid should move into the water, which will help avoid needless fall damage.

Here are the abilities to watch for:

* **[Frost Breath](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=21099/frost-breath)**: Frontal cone, also slows and drains mana. Keep the boss facing away from the raid.
* **[Gahz'ranka Slam](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24326/gahzranka-slam)**: Knocks back the primary threat target.
* **[Massive Geyser](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=22421/massive-geyser)**: Nearby players are launched into the air and threat is dropped on them. Land in the water to avoid fall damage. Tanks must quickly re-establish threat.
