# High Priest Mar'li
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

Very simple fight.

Here are the abilities to watch for:

## Troll Form

* **Spawns of Mar'li**: Mar'li will occasionally summon spider adds throughout the fight. These grow in power the longer they're left alive, so cleave them down.
* **[Drain Life](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24300/drain-life)**: Siphons health from a player to Mar'li. Interrupt.
* **[Poison Bolt Volley](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24099/poison-bolt-volley)**: Poisons everyone within 30yd. Casters and healers should stay max range during this fight to avoid it.

## Spider Form

* **[Enveloping Webs](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24110/enveloping-webs)**: Upon entering her Spider Form, she will drop all threat and root all players in her direct vicinity with webs. The webs also silence.
