# Jin'do the Hexxer
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

Here are the abilities to watch for:

* **Shades of Jin'do**: Shades will spawn in the shadow world that attack and interrupt players. These can only be seen and killed when under the affects of...
* **[Delusions of Jin'do](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24306/delusions-of-jindo)**: A DoT that gets applied to players that also grants them access to the shadow world. Players with this debuff should switch to killing shades, as they attack and interrupt all players. While this is a dispellable curse, it should typically *not* be dispelled. The damage is easy to outheal.
* **[Brain Wash Totem](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24262)**: Jin'do summons a totem that mind controls players. Due to the power level of each individual player in SoD, this is the most important mechanic in this fight now - destroy these totems ASAP.
* **[Powerful Healing Ward](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24309)**: Jin'do spawns a Healing Ward for himself. Lower prio than the brain wash totem, but still kill it ASAP.
* **[Hex](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24053/hex)**: CCs the current highest threat target. Magic dispel (Priests, Paladins, Arcane Mages).
* **Teleport**: Jin'do will occasionally teleport a random player into the nearby skeleton pit. Adds will spawn and attack them; AoE them down.
