# High Priest Venoxis
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

Very few abilities on this boss are even worth mentioning, but there are a couple.

## Troll Form

* **[Holy Wrath](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=23979/holy-wrath)**: A chain lightning that deals more damage each time it jumps to another target. Ranged need to be spread while he's in Troll form, just in case this goes out, or else they will die. He cannot cast this in Snake Form. The jump range is 5yd.

{: .tip .fs-3}
In Vanilla, melee would typically stay out for the entirety of Troll Form so they wouldn't die to Holy Wrath. In SoD you can typically burn the boss down fast enough that he enters Snake Form long before he's able to cast Holy Wrath. Ranged ought to spread anyway though, just in case.

## Snake Form

His snake form is far more damaging towards the tanks; focus heals on them.

* **[Enrage](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=8269)**: Venoxis enrages at 20%.
