# Brood Lord Mandokir
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

Mandokir spawns alongside his raptor, Ohgan. Ohgan will need to be offtanked separately. All DPS should focus Mandokir.

Here are the abilities to watch for:

## Mandokir

* **[Threatening Gaze](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24314)**: Mandokir will randomly target a player in the raid. This player must do **absolutely nothing** (stop attacking, healing, etc) until it's over. If they do not, Mandokir will charge at them and seriously hurt them.
* **[Mortal Strike](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=16856)**: Debuffs the tank, reducing healing by 50% for 5 sec. This one cannot be dispelled.
* **[Whirlwind](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=13736)**: Damages nearby players. Melee should run out during this.
* **[Charge](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24408)**: Mandokir randomly charges at players in the raid. When this occurs, it reduces threat on his current target, so tanks will need to taunt him to pick him back up.
* **[Enrage](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24318)**: If Ohgan dies while Mandokir is still alive, he will become permanently enraged.

## Ohgan

* **[Sunder Armor](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24317)**: Reduces armor on the offtank, capping at -5000 armor. In SoD this isn't really a big deal, and Mandokir is likely to die before you get close to full stacks anyway.
* **[Thrash](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=3391)**: Used very infrequently, but can occasionally burst the offtank.
