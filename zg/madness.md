# Edge of Madness
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

{: .important .fs-3}
Activating this encounter requires a **[Gurubashi Mojo Madness](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item=19931/gurubashi-mojo-madness)**.

In Season of Discovery, the Edge of Madness has been reworked. This encounter normally holds one of four bosses, and the boss spawned varies depending on the week, with a 2 week alternating schedule. This means that in Vanilla, it takes a minimum of 6 weeks (1, 2, 2, 1) to build your trinket.

In SoD, you can instead summon any of the four bosses by activating their respective plaque before using your Mojo Madness on the brazier.

The four possible bosses are listed below.

---

## Gri'lek

* **[Avatar](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24646/avatar)**: Gri'lek will become massive, drop all threat, and begin chasing a random player. He must be kited by his current target while everyone else burns him. If he reaches you, it's almost a guaranteed death.

---

## Hazza'rah

* **[Mana Burn](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=26046/mana-burn)**: Burns the mana of nearby players. Keep his mana drained to prevent this from casting.
* **[Summon Nightmare Illusions](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24729/summon-nightmare-illusions)**: Hazza'rah summons a bunch of adds that deal high damage but have almost no health. Focus them down.
* **[Sleep](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24664/sleep)**: Puts the raid to sleep for 6 seconds.

---

## Renataki

* **[Vanish](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24699/vanish)**: Renataki will stealth and must be revealed.
* **[Ambush](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24337/ambush)**: If left alone in stealth, Renataki will re-emerge and deal bonus damage to a player.

---

## Wushoolay

* **[Chain Lightning](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=23106/chain-lightning)**: Despite what it seems, this is actually directional. Keep the boss facing away from the raid and only the tank will be hit.
* **[Lightning Cloud](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=26550/lightning-cloud)**: Deals AoE damage over time; move out of it.
