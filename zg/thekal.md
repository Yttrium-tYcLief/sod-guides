# High Priest Thekal
*Last updated: 7/14/26*

This encounter begins with three mini-bosses: High Priest Thekal, Zealot Lor'Khan, and Zealot Zath. There are also two tiger trash mobs. All three mini-bosses must be killed within 15sec of each other to progress to Phase 2.

Here are the mini-boss abilities:

### **High Priest Thekal**:

* **[Mortal Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=22859)**: Not actually a cleave; this is an un-dispellable Mortal Strike, reducing healing received by the tank by 50% for 5 sec.
* **[Silence](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=27559)**: Silences a nearby player for 3 sec.

### <span class="class-shaman">Zealot Lor'Khan</span>:

* **[Great Heal](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=25807)**: Heals an ally.
* **[Lightning Shield](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=23552)**: Damages those who hit him.
* **[Disarm](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=22691/disarm)**: Forces a target to temporarily unequip their weapon.
* **[Bloodlust](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24185/bloodlust)**: Boosts the attack speed of a single ally by 75%.

### <span class="class-rogue">Zealot Zath</span>:

* **[Sweeping Strikes](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=26654/sweeping-strikes)**: He cleaves.
* **[Gouge](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24698/gouge)**: Stuns a player for 4 sec.
* **[Blind](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=21060/blind)**: Dazes a target for 10 sec.
* **[Kick](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=27613/kick)**: Interrupts spellcasting.

Once all three mini-bosses are dead, Thekal will shapeshift into his **Tiger Form**. In this form, he has new abilities:

* **[Force Punch](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24189)**: Thekal will knock back all nearby players.
* **[Summon Zulian Guardians](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24183)**: Periodically, Thekal will summon a pair of tiger adds. Stack and cleave.
* **[Speed Slash](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=24192)**: Simple DoT.
* **[Enrage](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=8269)**: At 20% health, Thekal will Enrage, which persists until he dies.
