The first boss is inside the Cathedral, but the door is locked.

There are minibosses (Captains) littered about. You do not have to kill all Captains to summon the first boss. But if you do, Captains are Paladins. They change Pally auras. When ret aura, does lots of thorn damage. When devo aura, has +2490 spell resist.

Praetorians Whirlwind - avoid. They also have a mechanic that does a massive AoE blast, choreographed as a blue circle emanating from them, roughly 10 yds. Cannot be kicked.

Paladins throw down a red Consecrate (retail visual). Massive AoE damage, avoid.

Fight your way to the top of the Abbey and ring the bell at the top.

Miniboss Captain Karnwield spawns in front of Cath when Abbey bell rung.

After Karnwield dies, enter the Cathedral and confront Boss 1. They fly out of Cath as a shade and go to the Fountain in central Tyr's Hand. First boss fight occurs there. When they spawn, they'll kill any remaining trash.
