The first boss is inside the Cathedral, but the door is locked.

There are minibosses (Captains) littered about. You must kill all Captains to unlock the Cathedral and summon the first boss.

Captains are Paladins. They change Pally auras. When ret aura, does lots of thorn damage. When devo aura, has +2490 spell resist.

First kill Captain Buzan in front of the Cathedral, he's in the first few packs.

Praetorians Whirlwind - avoid. They also have a mechanic that does a massive AoE blast, choreographed as a blue circle emanating from them, roughly 10 yds. Cannot be kicked.

Next go to Barracks and kill Captain Manadar in top room.

Next go to Abbey and ring the bell at the top.

Inside Abbey, some mobs put down a giant red Consecrate with a more well-defined circle AoE, avoid!

Nothing in the back wings of the Abbey.

Miniboss Captain Karnwield spawns in front of Cath when Abbey bell rung.

After Karnwield dies, enter the Cathedral and confront Boss 1. They fly out of Cath as a shade and go to the Fountain in central Tyr's Hand. First boss fight occurs there.
