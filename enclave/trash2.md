You'll begin in the Barracks in southern New Avalon. Fight your way outside, and fight your way to the town square. You'll need to kill the other Scarlet leader mini-bosses in order to spawn Boss 2:

* Cardinal Stiltz, in the nearby Chapel
* Bowmaster Puck, at the Training Range
* Arcanist Hilda, in the Mage Tower
* Knight-Captain Fratley, in the Scarlet Hold (Barracks)
* Shield Warden Stein, at the top of the Scout Tower

Then, a large Scarlet regiment will spawn in the town square, coming from the direction of Tyr's Hand.
