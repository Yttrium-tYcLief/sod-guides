You'll begin in the basement prison of the Barracks in southern New Avalon. An Argent Dawn member will pick the lock so you can escape. Talk to the Night Elf named Fydriel near the Barracks exit - he will tell you which of the next minibosses are empowered.

Fight your way outside, and fight your way to the town square. You'll need to fight all 6 listed below in order to spawn the next boss. 

Whichever 2 were mentioned as being empowered cannot be killed, and will simply flee when fought. They will show up in the Boss 2 encounter.

Whichever 2 you fight first that are *not* empowered will die, and will *not* show up in the Boss 2 encounter.

Whichever 2 you fight last will simply flee when fought, and show up in the Boss 2 encounter, just like the empowered ones.

Which ones are empowered will be random each week, but consistent across all raids for that week, similar to BWL's weekly required affix. This means that each week, you will be forced to have two specific leaders in the fight, but which two out of four of the remaining leaders are in the fight is up to you.

I guess you can think of it like a pick/ban system. Blizzard picks 2, and then you can ban 2, and Blizzard picks the remaining 2.

If you need to adjust your kill order/selection, you can soft reset the instance. This means everyone must zone out and then the raid leader uses the "Reset All Instances" button. **All trash within New Avalon will fully respawn.**

The leaders of the various infantry are as follows:

* Cardinal Stiltz, in the nearby Chapel
* Bowmaster Puck, at the Training Range (near the Orchard)
* Knight-Captain Fratley, in front of the Scarlet Hold Barracks
* Shield Warden Stein, atop the Watch Tower
* Arcanist Hilda, at the top of the Mage Tower
* Cannon Mistress Lind, in the Forge

Additionally, an enemy named Cardinal Martell seems to exist within the Town Hall, but killing him does nothing and the Town Hall does not burn like the other 6 buildings/areas.

Once all 6 have been fought (2 are dead and 4 have fled), a large Scarlet regiment will spawn in the town square, coming from the direction of Tyr's Hand. Approach it to begin the Boss 2 encounter.
