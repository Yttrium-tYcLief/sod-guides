*Last updated: 5/9/25*

Once Bosses 3, 4, and 5 are all dead, head to the New Avalon Monastery to the northwest. Outside you'll find a friendly Mage named Angela Dosantos - talk to her to open a portal into the Monastery. Once inside, fight your way forward until you reach Boss 6.

There are a few minibosses you'll fight along the way:

### [Archmagus Nilas Arcanister](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/npc=241665/archmagus-nilas-arcanister)
Basically identical to Tenn Flamecaster, who was standing outside of Solistrasza's arena.
* **[Wyvern's Breath](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=1231249)**: Causes him to slowly spin in place and deal damage to anyone within a short cone in front of him. 
* **[Fire Torrent](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=1233570)**: He rolls 5 lava boulders away from him, avoid.

He also uses other Mage abilities like Fireball, Arcane Explosion, etc, but these do relatively minor damage.

### [Commander Granis Darkhammer](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/npc=240803/commander-granis-darkhammer)
This miniboss is entirely unique.
* **[Holy Cleave](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=1234894)**: He cleaves targets close to his primary target, so don't stand near the tank.
* **[Reckoning](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=1233507)**: Holy circles will orbit around the area. These are in groups of 3, and orbit the point around which they spawn, slowly moving outwards as they do. Avoid these circles or take damage.
* **[Righteous Reckoning](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/spell=1233442)**: As his health gets lower he'll start summoning holy hammers in the sky that slam into the ground. When they impact, they explode and deal holy damage to nearby players. Avoid these as well.

His abilities cease to deal damage once he dies.
